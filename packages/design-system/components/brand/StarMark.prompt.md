The eight-point sparkle mark. Use as an app icon, favicon, loading glyph, or a quiet accent between sections.

```jsx
<StarMark size={28} tone="clay" />
<StarMark size={20} title="NX" />   {/* labelled = non-decorative */}
```

- `size`: square px (default 24)
- `tone`: `"ink"` · `"dusty"` · `"clay"` · `"offwhite"` · `"current"`
