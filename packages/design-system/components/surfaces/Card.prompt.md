The base Leida surface, soft, generously rounded (26px). Paper, frosted glass, tile, or ink.

```jsx
<Card>Default paper card</Card>
<Card variant="glass" padding="lg">Frosted panel over texture</Card>
<Card variant="ink">Inverse card</Card>
<Card variant="tile" hoverLift>Lifts on hover</Card>
```
- `variant`: `"paper"` · `"glass"` · `"tile"` · `"ink"`
- `padding`: `"sm"` · `"md"` · `"lg"` or any CSS value
- `hoverLift` for the product-card rise
